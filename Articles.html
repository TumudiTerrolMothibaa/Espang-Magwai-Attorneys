Opening of limpopo deeds registry

  June 6, 2017

3 April 2017 @ 08:00 - The moment etched in our memories as Espag Magwai made history by lodging the first transaction in the Limpopo Deeds Office.

Prior to this date, all conveyancing matters within the Limpopo province were attended to by the Pretoria Deeds Office. The legal position has now changed, and the Limpopo Deeds Office has exclusive jurisdiction over property matters within the Province. This means faster turn-around times and less costs for local attorneys.

Mr Rhemtula from Espag Magwai executed their first deed before the Registrar of Deeds shortly after the opening of the Limpopo Deeds Office.

“This is a major milestone for our Province and the legal profession, and we are elated to execute our own documents at the Deeds Office” said Rhemtula in an exclusive interview.

Best wishes to William Ngququ (Registrar of Deeds Limpopo) and his team, who worked tirelessly to ensure the successful and smooth transition, opening and operating of the Limpopo Deeds Office despite all the negativity from the pessimists.
